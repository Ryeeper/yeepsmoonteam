Project Description

The yeeps moon team, is a special team of people simmilar to YWDS gathered for building. But instead of building official maps we are looking to create the best maps possible, ones that push the boundrays of Difficulty,Spectacle, and Fun

We are currently in the very early stages of development, mainly setting up the groundwork, and planning the project, there are currently no applications but if you would like to join contact @Ryeeper
